Our Story

Bringing world-class barbering to the heart of Letlhakane

Precision. Passion.
People.

Factory Fades was founded with one simple mission: to deliver exceptional modern hairstyles in a welcoming, professional environment right here in Letlhakane.

What started as a small vision next to the Bus Rank has quickly become the go-to destination for men, women, students, and families who want quality cuts without the city prices or long waits.
