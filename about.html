Our Story

Factory Fades is a modern barbershop located in Letlhakane, next to the Bus Rank. We provide quality haircuts for men and women of all ages.

Our mission is to deliver fresh, modern styles in a clean and welcoming environment. We believe every client deserves to leave our shop feeling confident and looking their best.

From skin fades to ladies cuts, our skilled barbers are trained in the latest techniques to ensure you always get the best results.
